# Superstore-Sales-Analysis

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales performance and profitability using Python. 
The analysis explores how sales, profit, and discount strategies impact overall business performance, along with regional and category-level trends.

## Key Focus Areas
- Analysis of overall sales and profit trends
- Evaluation of discount impact on profitability and profit margins
- Category-wise and sub-category-wise performance analysis
- Regional comparison of sales and profit contribution
- Identification of key business insights through visual analysis

## Key Insights
- Sales growth does not always translate into proportional profit growth.
- Higher discount levels have a negative impact on profit margins.
- Profit contribution varies significantly across categories and regions.
- A small number of categories and products contribute a large share of total revenue and profit.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas (data manipulation and aggregation)
- Matplotlib (data visualization)
